About me

I’ve worked as a beat reporter, long-form feature writer, editor in chief, business operations manager, general manager of an interior design technology company, and now as a marketing director. Along the way, I’ve collaborated with web developers and programmers and often wondered how the guts of the properties and applications they built worked. I felt like I made so many decisions about how software, web applications and user experiences would functions, but never actually built the things myself.

Now, I’ll learn how to. I am studying programming from the ground up, starting with markup languages and moving on to Javascript and beyond. I can’t say for sure what my specialization will be in, but I want to make beautiful, useful web and mobile applications, and leverage my “softer” skills — creative writing, graphic design, understanding how non-technical people want to interact with software — along the way.

Here you’ll find examples of my programming work and my design and writing projects. I hope you find your time here useful.
